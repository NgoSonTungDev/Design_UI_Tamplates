LAMBORGHINI

Lamborghini pre-owned cars in the Selezione program are covered by an additional
Lamborghini warranty ranging from a minimum of 12 to a maximum of 24 months.
The warranty covers parts and labor and ensures that all operations are carried
out by qualified Lamborghini technicians using only original components.
